Sports has always been the great source of entertainment from ages. It is a debatable topic that whether the rich people follow sports more or whether poor people follow it more. This scenario tries to compare the number of sports tweets(AFL) in the city with the financial status of the people in that city.

Map Representing the analysis


Chart Representing the analysis
As we can observe that more number of sports tweets are coming from Melbourne while the least number of tweets are coming from Darwin. Darwin has the highest average monthly income among the Australian cities, but the number of sports tweets coming in from Darwin is substantially less. This can be because Darwin doesn't have an AFL team. We can state that the income in the city is not directly proportional to the number of sports enthusiasts in the city.
