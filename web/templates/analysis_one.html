I love tweet in the Morning


People are known to have different moods during different times of the day. In order to find out whether people are more sad in morning or night, the percentage of sad tweets vs the part of the day analysis is performed. The time of the day was divided into three parts: 4.00 am to 11.59 noon, 12.00 noon to 07.59 pm, and 08.00 pm to 03.59 am.
As we can observe from the graph, the rate of sad tweets is more in morning as compared to any other time of the day. City wise, Darwin has been the city with highest rate of negative tweets. Hobart has the lowest rate of sad tweets among all the cities. The rate of sad tweets can be seen decreasing as the day progresses.
